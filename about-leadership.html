Four Wards. One Movement. One Leader.

MFA Ambassadors is structured across all four wards of Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I — Ward A, B, E, and F — with a clear chain of leadership from the Principal down to our ground-level coordinators and executive team.

We Are a Structure

Not a Crowd — A Coordinated Force

The MFA Ambassadors are not a loosely assembled crowd. Every Ambassador is drawn from the communities of Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I, with clear roles and a shared conviction: MFA's continued representation means progress for every community across all four wards. Our slogan says it all — "One With God Is A Majority — Ileri Oluwa Dan Dan Ni."
