Who He Is

A Legislator Rooted in Decades of Service

Rt. Hon. Mojeed Fatai Adebola — popularly known as MFA — is a four-term Member of the Lagos State House of Assembly representing Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I under the All Progressives Congress (APC). He currently serves as the Chief Whip of the House, a position that places him at the heart of legislative coordination in Lagos State.

From humble beginnings in Bogije, Ibeju-Lekki, MFA rose through academic excellence, public service in local government, and four continuous terms of legislative achievement to become one of the most impactful legislators in Lagos State history. His tenure has delivered over ₦50 billion in infrastructure value, 2,400+ JAMB forms, multiple road projects, and economic empowerment programmes across all four wards of Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I.

He holds a Masters in Business Administration & Finance from LAUTECH and is currently pursuing a Doctorate in Business Administration (DBA) at Ajayi Crowther University, Oyo — a man who never stopped learning while serving his people.

Legislative Record

Four Terms of Delivery

What He Has Achieved for Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I

1st Term · 2011–2015

Building the Foundation

Vice Chairman, House Committee on House Services & Special Duties. Established his legislative presence and laid groundwork for constituency development.

2nd Term · 2015–2019

Education & Roads

Chairman, House Committee on Transportation. Delivered free school buses, road grading projects, community boreholes, and free JAMB forms — a landmark educational intervention for the constituency.

3rd Term · 2019–2023

Welfare & Empowerment

Chairman, Public Accounts (Local) Committee. Sponsored Mecca and Jerusalem pilgrimages, distributed electricity transformers, provided SME capital support, and created direct employment opportunities.

4th Term · 2023–2027 (Current)

Infrastructure Surge

Chief Whip & Rules and Protocol Committee. Delivering 10 road construction projects across Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I, with total infrastructure value exceeding ₦50 billion. Continued JAMB & economic empowerment programmes.

The Vision

His Vision for Continuity

MFA's mission is built on continuity and expansion. The work is not finished. Roads still need to be completed. Communities still need a strong, experienced voice in the Lagos State House of Assembly. As Chief Whip, his influence secures resources for Ibeju-Lekki that other constituencies can only aspire to. The MFA Ambassadors carry this message across all four wards — Ward A, B, E, and F — of Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I.

"One With God Is A Majority. We are not just campaigning — we are carrying a community mandate back to the House of Assembly."
— Rt. Hon. Mojeed Fatai Adebola (MFA)
