Who We Are

The MFA Ambassadors

We are a people-first, community-rooted political movement. Established as the official grassroots mobilisation and media arm of Rt. Hon. Mojeed Fatai Adebola — Chief Whip of the Lagos State House of Assembly and four-term Member representing Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I under the APC.

We are not a crowd. We are a structure. Every Ambassador is chosen from Ward E communities, with clear roles and a shared conviction: MFA's return to the House means victory for Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I.

Our work spans community mobilisation, digital communications, and APC alignment. From town hall meetings to WhatsApp broadcasts, we ensure MFA's voice reaches every household in Ward E.

Community Mobilisation — Ward E Coverage

Campaign carried to every corner of Ward E, engaging all 12 communities.

  • Abijo Town: Door-to-door community sensitisation, voter engagement, and political awareness sessions. Residents were briefed on MFA's legislative record and his vision for a 5th term.
  • Owode Labora, Abule Oluwa & Mushikogo Towns: Coordinated town meetings with community leaders and residents. The Ambassadors listened to community concerns and channelled feedback into the campaign's planning structure.
  • Iwerekun, Gbetu I & II Towns: Ambassador network deployed for ward-level political awareness. Iwerekun, in particular, carries strong ties to the broader Ibeju-Lekki alumni and civic community — and the engagement there was deep and well-received.
  • Awoyaya I, II & Abule Parapo Towns: Structured town hall-style consultations were held. Campaign materials were distributed. Ward-level leaders were aligned with the Ambassadors' operational structure.
  • Eputu & Ogunfayo Towns: Community engagement sessions focused on reaffirming MFA's record on constituency development — roads, education, representation, and welfare — and building renewed commitment ahead of the election.
  • Adeba, Lakowe & Golf/Onishon Towns: The final corridor of Ward E coverage. The Adeba, Lakowe, and Golf Town communities received direct Ambassador visits, campaign materials, and one-on-one conversations with community leaders.

Purpose & Values

What We Stand For

Anchored in the conviction that Rt. Hon. Mojeed Fatai Adebola's return benefits every resident of Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I. Our work drives four core pillars.

1. Grassroots Mobilisation

We go where the people are, town by town, ensuring every voter knows MFA's name and record.

2. Political Awareness

MFA's four-term record in roads, education, welfare, and representation is made known as real stories.

3. Strategic Alignment

Coordinated with APC at ward, local, and state levels for disciplined, unified results.

4. Digital Communication

Weekly WhatsApp broadcasts and social media reach every screen and conversation.

More than a campaign team — a replicable model for community-driven political communication across Epe Division. Built on June 13, 2025, and growing stronger.
