About Us

Who We Are

The MFA Ambassadors

We are a people-first, community-rooted political movement inspired by the leadership, vision, and progressive values of Rt. Hon. Mojeed Fatai Adebola, Chief Whip of the Lagos State House of Assembly, representing Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I under the APC.

Every Ambassador is chosen from Ibeju/Lekki communities with a shared conviction: Promoting good governance in Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I. Our work spans community mobilisation, digital communications, and APC alignment.
