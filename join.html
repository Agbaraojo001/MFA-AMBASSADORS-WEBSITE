Become an MFA Ambassador

Join hundreds of committed community voices from Wards A, B, E & F. Register today, receive your official Ambassador code, and stand with Hon. Mojeed's mandate of service and progress.

Why Register?

What You Get as an Ambassador

  • ✓ Official Ambassador Status — Receive a unique registration code that recognises you as a certified MFA Ambassador for Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I.
  • ✓ Ward Representation — Be a recognised voice for your ward in Constituency I's grassroots network, directly aligned with APC's agenda.
  • ✓ First Access to Events — Get early invitations to endorsement gatherings, mobilisation drives, and community development programmes.
  • ✓ Community Leadership — Take an active role in shaping the future of your community through Hon. Mojeed's four-term mandate of service.
  • ✓ Digital Recognition — Be part of the MFA Ambassadors' growing digital presence across social media and community platforms.

All four wards of Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I are covered: Ward A, Ward B, Ward E & Ward F. Your VIN (Voter Identification Number) is required to verify your registration in the constituency.
