Our work on the ground — community-by-community engagement across all 12 communities of Ward E, Ibeju-Lekki.

Community Mobilisation

Ward E Coverage

Every Community. Every Household.

The campaign has been carried to every corner of Ward E. Ambassador teams were deployed to engage residents, collect feedback, distribute campaign materials, and ensure MFA's legislative record and fifth-term vision were communicated directly and honestly.

  • Abijo Town — Door-to-door community sensitisation, voter engagement, and political awareness sessions. Residents were briefed on MFA's legislative record and his vision for a 5th term.
  • Owode Labora, Abule Oluwa & Mushikogo Towns — Coordinated town meetings with community leaders and residents. The Ambassadors listened to community concerns and channelled feedback into the campaign's planning structure.
  • Iwerekun, Gbetu I & II Towns — Ambassador network deployed for ward-level political awareness. Iwerekun, in particular, carries strong ties to the broader Ibeju-Lekki alumni and civic community — engagement was deep and well-received.
  • Awoyaya I, II & Abule Parapo Towns — Structured town hall-style consultations held. Campaign materials distributed. Ward-level leaders aligned with the Ambassadors' operational structure.
  • Eputu & Ogunfayo Towns — Community engagement sessions focused on reaffirming MFA's record on constituency development: roads, education, representation, and welfare — building renewed commitment ahead of the election.
  • Adeba, Lakowe & Golf/Onishon Towns — The final corridor of Ward E coverage. Direct Ambassador visits, campaign materials, and one-on-one conversations with community leaders. Ward E fully covered.
Campaign Activities

What We Have Done

Campaign Activities Summary

  • Inauguration Flag-Off — June 13, 2025, in the presence of the Executive Chairman and APC leaders at the official launch ceremony.
  • 12-Community Town-to-Town Mobilisation — Ward E fully covered with direct Ambassador engagement in every community.
  • 4,000 Campaign Posters — Formally presented to the Executive Chairman at the Council Secretariat, signalling the scale and seriousness of the campaign.
  • Courtesy Visit to the Executive Chairman's Office — Flag-bearer unveiling announced, strengthening party alignment and demonstrating unity.
  • Weekly WhatsApp Broadcast Programme — Ongoing, reaching thousands across the constituency every week with campaign updates and MFA's message.
  • Social Media Campaigns — Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter) content consistently maintained by the Media & Communication directorate.
  • Community Feedback Collection — Documented at every engagement and integrated directly into campaign strategy and planning.
