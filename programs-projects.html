4th Term Road Projects

Roads That Connect Communities

Under MFA's 4th term as Chief Whip, 10 road construction projects have been initiated across Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I — spanning all four wards. Below is the full status of every project.

Completed Projects

Awoyaya–Eputu Road

Linking two of the constituency's major communities with improved road surface, drainage, and access.

Completed

Lakowe Phase II Road (~2km)

Second phase of road development in Lakowe community, approximately 2 kilometres completed.

Completed

Onishon Community Road (850m)

850-metre road serving the Golf/Onishon community area, opening access previously hindered by poor road conditions.

Completed

Alabede/Fire II Road, Bogije (Solar Streetlights)

Road construction in Bogije with solar-powered streetlights — improving night safety and community visibility.

Completed
Flagged Off

Onosa Road (4.2km)

4.2-kilometre road officially flagged off by Rt. Hon. MFA, connecting Onosa community to the wider road network.

Flagged Off
In Progress

Kajola–Bogije Road (2km+) — 50%+ Complete

Over 2 kilometres linking Kajola to Bogije, currently more than 50% completed. A critical arterial road for the Ward E corridor.

In Progress

Bogije–Malete/Shapati Road (4km+) — 2.5km Done

4-kilometre+ road with 2.5km already completed. Includes solar streetlights along completed sections, serving the Malete and Shapati communities.

In Progress

Alakun/Baba Adisa Road

Road project connecting 5 communities in the Alakun/Baba Adisa corridor — one of the most impactful multi-community connectivity projects this term.

In Progress

Ibeju-Agbe – Iba Road

Strategic road linking schools and the palace in the Ibeju-Agbe to Iba corridor, improving access to education and community institutions.

In Progress

Okegun Eleku – Ogunlana – Alausa Road

Spanning three distinct neighbourhoods — Okegun Eleku, Ogunlana, and Alausa — improving intra-community connectivity across the entire corridor.

In Progress
Planned (2026 Appropriation)

New Community Roads — 2026 Budget

Additional road projects across new communities in Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I earmarked in the 2026 Appropriation Bill, pending construction commencement.

Planned

Our Latest Event

April 2026 · Ibeju-Lekki

"From Grassroots to Greatness" Symposium

On 4th April 2026, MFA Ambassadors hosted the landmark "From Grassroots to Greatness" symposium at the NJS Royale Event and Conference Centre, Ibeju-Lekki — bringing together community leaders, stakeholders, and supporters from all four wards.

The event celebrated over a decade of constituency development, showcasing completed road projects, the JAMB scheme's impact, economic empowerment testimonies, and the vision for continued growth across Ibeju-Lekki Constituency I.
